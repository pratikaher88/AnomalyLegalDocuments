open source software in products
products include components subject to terms conditions of open source software licenses
to extent applicable we will identify open source software included in product in through product itself
some of these licenses require us to provide open source software to you on terms of open source license instead of terms of agreement
in that case terms of open source license will apply you will have rights granted in such licenses to open source software itself such as access to source code right to make modifications right to reverse engineer

combining products with open source software
requirement of some open source licenses sometimes known as copyleft licenses is that any modifications to open source software combinations of open source software with other software such as by linking must be made available in source code form under terms of copyleft license
examples of copyleft licenses include gpl lgpl affero cpl cddl eclipse mozilla licenses
to extent you are separately authorized by atlassian to combine distribute products with any other code you must make sure that your use does not i impose give appearance of imposing any condition obligation on us with respect to our products including without limitation any obligation to distribute our products under open source license ii grant appear to grant to any third party any rights to immunities under our intellectual property proprietary rights in our products
to be clear you may not combine otherwise modify our products unless we expressly give you right to do so under this agreement
