this guide describes information that github needs in order to process counter notice to dmca takedown request
if you have more general questions about what dmca is how github processes dmca takedown requests please review our dmca takedown policy
if you believe your content on github was mistakenly disabled by dmca takedown request you have right to contest takedown by submitting counter notice
if you do we will wait days then re enable your content unless copyright owner initiates legal action against you before then
our counter notice form set forth below is consistent with form suggested by dmca statute which can be found at u s
copyright office s official website http www copyright gov
as with all legal matters it is always best to consult with professional about your specific questions situation
we strongly encourage you to do so before taking any action that might impact your rights
this guide isn t legal advice shouldn t be taken as such
tell truth
dmca requires that you swear to your counter notice under penalty of perjury
it is federal crime to intentionally lie in sworn declaration
see u s code title section
submitting false information could also result in civil liability that is you could get sued for money damages
investigate
submitting dmca counter notice can have real legal consequences
if complaining party disagrees that their takedown notice was mistaken they might decide to file lawsuit against you to keep content disabled
you should conduct thorough investigation into allegations made in takedown notice probably talk to lawyer before submitting counter notice
you must have good reason to submit counter notice
in order to file counter notice you must have good faith belief that material was removed disabled as result of mistake misidentification of material to be removed disabled
u s code title section g
whether you decide to explain why you believe there was mistake is up to you your lawyer but you do need to identify mistake before you submit counter notice
in past we have received counter notices citing mistakes in takedown notice such as complaining party doesn t have copyright i have license code has been released under open source license that permits my use complaint doesn t account for fact that my use is protected by fair use doctrine
of course there could be other defects with takedown notice
copyright laws are complicated
sometimes takedown notice might allege infringement in way that seems odd indirect
copyright laws are complicated can lead to some unexpected results
in some cases takedown notice might allege that your source code infringes because of what it can do after it is compiled run
for example
these are just few examples of complexities of copyright law
since there are many nuances to law some unsettled questions in these types of cases it is especially important to get professional advice if infringement allegations do not seem straightforward
your counter notice will be published
as noted in our dmca takedown policy after redacting personal information we publish all complete actionable counter notices at https github com github dmca
additional resources
if you need additional help there are many self help resources online
chilling effects has informative set of faqs about copyright dmca safe harbor
if you are involved with open source project in need of legal advice you can contact software freedom law center
if you think you have particularly challenging case non profit organizations such as electronic frontier foundation may also be willing to help directly refer you to lawyer
include following statement i have read understand github s guide to filing dmca counter notice
we won t refuse to process otherwise complete counter notice if you don t include this statement however we will know that you haven t read these guidelines may ask you to go back do so
identify content that was disabled location where it appeared
disabled content should have been identified by url in takedown notice
you simply need to copy url s that you want to challenge
provide your contact information
include your email address name telephone number physical address
include following statement i swear under penalty of perjury that i have good faith belief that material was removed disabled as result of mistake misidentification of material to be removed disabled
you may also choose to communicate reasons why you believe there was mistake misidentification
if you think of your counter notice as note to complaining party this is chance to explain why they should not take next step file lawsuit in response
this is yet another reason to work with lawyer when submitting counter notice
include following statement i consent to jurisdiction of federal district court for judicial district in which my address is located if in united states otherwise northern district of california where github is located i will accept service of process from person who provided dmca notification agent of such person
include your physical electronic signature
fastest way to get response is to send email notification to copyright github com
you may include attachment if you like but please also include plain text version of your letter in body of your message
if you must send your notice by physical mail you can do that too but it will take substantially longer for us to receive respond to it day waiting period starts from when we receive your counter notice
notices we receive via plain text email have much faster turnaround than pdf attachments physical mail
if you still wish to mail us your notice our physical address is
