any capitalized terms used but not defined below have meanings in agreement
purpose of this supplement is to allow your customers to be authorized users subject to terms below

if you designate any of your customers as authorized users you will be responsible for their use of software hosted services as you would be for any other authorized user
accordingly you must enter into valid binding agreements with your customers that require them to comply with applicable terms of agreement including this supplement including any conditions restrictions with respect to use of access to software hosted services

for avoidance of doubt allowing your customers to be authorized users does not mean that you may distribute resell oem any software hosted services make products available to your customers on standalone basis
rather it means only that your customers may access your instance of software hosted services in order to support their use of your own products services that are unrelated to software hosted services your products

examples include
your customer accessing your confluence instance to view customer facing knowledge base articles technical documentation about your products that you have posted in your confluence instance
you using your confluence instance as customer facing extranet for discussions project updates centralized knowledge sharing source about your products
your customers accessing your jira instance to provide you with customer feedback on development of your products
you using your jira service desk instance as customer facing helpdesk support system allowing customers to access your jira instance to open support tickets

you may not charge your customers for any use of software hosted service itself but may charge your customers as part of overall program that includes access to your instance of software hosted service
example would be charging your customers for your product where your product is paid support offering that includes as minor component access to support forum which you provide using your confluence instance

as authorized user your customers may interact with software hosted service but may not receive any administrator configuration similar access to product
for example your customers may post comments in jira issue but you may not permit any of your customers to administer project global site level configurations

atlassian will not have any direct indirect liability obligation to any of your customers
for avoidance of doubt your customers are not parties to third party beneficiaries under agreement

you will defend indemnify hold harmless atlassian from against any loss cost liability damage including attorneys fees for which atlassian becomes liable arising from relating to any claim brought against us by your customers arising out of their use of software hosted services through you
